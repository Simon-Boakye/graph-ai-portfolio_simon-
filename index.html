The Mission

To leverage technology responsibly in solving complex health challenges, strengthening immunization equity, and optimizing research workflows in resource-limited settings.

About Me

I am a Licensed Public Health Officer (Disease Control) and a Master of Science in Global Health candidate at the University of Geneva.

My background includes hands-on field experience in new vaccine introduction, immunization campaigns, and outbreak response. I am now focused on applying Generative AI to accelerate research and improve scientific communication.

Key Competencies

  • Epidemiology
  • Data Analysis (STATA, R)
  • Vaccine Introduction
  • Global Health Policy
  • AI Workflow Automation

Course Modules

Websites & Docs
